We couldn’t find a pharmacy experience worthy of our best buds— so we created one.

  • Patients first, always

    We know how stressful it can be when your pet needs meds. That’s why we offer free next-day delivery and even do same-day in NYC and LA. We got you.

  • Tailored care

    We’ll work with you and your veterinarian to find the very best solution for your pet. With our custom compounded options, we can change the dosage, flavor and form. We even have a flavor guarantee with over 40 options! P.S. Everyone seems to love the chicken-flavored chew treats!

  • Support, 7 days a week

    You’re not alone. Text, call, or email us 7 days a week—we’ll answer and do our best to sprinkle in a bit of sunshine. ☀️

How it works

  • We receive your prescription

    Either your veterinarian sends us a prescription or we reach out to them for you. Whatever is easiest!

  • We deliver your care package

    Fast and free next-day delivery, straight to your door. Same-day in NYC and LA.

  • You treat your pet

    Give your pet the care they need to feel better. Plus they can play with our included toy!
